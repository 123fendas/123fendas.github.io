Super Mario Hammer

I bring to you Super Mario Hammer, with 8 worlds with 4 levels each.
I didn't consider the other characters, but I suppose you could play it with characters other than Mario if you want to.
Going back to the first level in a world if you get a game over is optional; if you want to play like that, I have sprinkled in a decent amount of coins and 1-ups in the levels.
Also, for you Koopas the Quick and Il Piantissimos out there, here's a fun challenge: my speed run record is 23:23.23.
