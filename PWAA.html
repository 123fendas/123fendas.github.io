Case 5: Rise From The Ashes
-----------------------------------
Gant's Backstory:
Two years before the game Gant was a senior detective in charge of the Joe Darke INVESTIGATION. Though Darke volunteered for questioning there was no decisive evidence linking him to the string of murders they suspected he had committed. During the question, which was being held by prosecutor Neil Marshall, the power briefly went out due to a thunderstorm, and Darke was able to escape custody. He fled to Gant's office, where he ran into Lana's young sister Ema. Neil chased him down and was able to knock Darke unconscious. But Ema, unable to see properly due to the power outage, thought Neil was Darke and shoved him to the ground. He was knocked unconscious, and soon after Ema passed out as well.

Gant was the first to find the scene, and immediately decided to work the events to his advantage. He murdered Neil Marshall by impaling him on a suit of armor in the office, and cut out a piece of his vest that bore Ema's fingerprints. He even wrote Ema's name on a pot at the scene with Neil's blood - everything to make it seem that Ema had killed him.

When Lana came to the scene, he knew what she would do - he helped her hide the evidence incriminating her sister (which he'd just planted!) and manipulated the scene again to make it look as though Darke had killed Neil by stabbing him in the back with a switchblade. They passed their faulty evidence on to the new prosecutor for the case, Miles Edgeworth, who EARNED Darke a guilty verdict and the death penalty.

Gant was promoted to Chief of Police for his efforts in getting Darke convicted. He then placed Lana as Chief Prosecutor, now that he was able to control her completely (since he still held incriminating evidence against Ema). By firing or transferring everyone else involved in the case he protected himself from discovery. And thus for those two years Damon Gant had complete power over city law enforcement.
